WE ARE MVMNT

For more than 40 years, MV Transportation has provided safe, reliable, affordable transportation services to cities, counties, municipalities, and other jurisdictional entities, as well as private corporations, non-profit agencies, and community organizations.

we serve

MV is your strategic transportation services partner. Driven by reliability and innovation, our key areas of expertise include:

We serve diverse communities—from small, rural fixed-route services to large, high-profile systems. Across these systems, our clients share the same goal: safe, reliable, predictable, easy-to-use, cost effective service.

MV works with colleges and universities to plan and implement transportation options that enhance campus life and improve the safety and mobility of students, faculty, and visitors.

MV works with corporations to enhance employee benefits and satisfaction with transportation solutions that encourage increased productivity and alleviate growing traffic congestion.

MV works with school districts in both urban and rural settings to deliver safe, secure, and reliable student transportation between home and school.

Solutions we deliver

We deliver one of the industry’s most comprehensive range of proven transportation solutions to meet your individual needs.

We offer a unique blend of on-demand ADA-compliant transportation services for urban and rural settings – to help you reduce costs and increase service.

We offer fixed-route, flex-route, commuter bus, and shuttle services – providing some of the largest privately-operated services in the nation.

We offer public, private and airport shuttle operations, connecting employees, students, and passengers to their places of work or desired destinations from commuter train stations, transfer centers, park-and-ride lots, and other origins.

We provide safe and reliable school bus operations, delivering expertise in both traditional and special needs transportation.

We have the right operational expertise and talented workforce to execute a plan that maximizes resources across all of your transportation service needs.

We offer specialized transit and technology consulting on demand management, route optimization, ridership planning, maintenance, and safety.

Transit Management Platforms

MV implements, enhances and maintains industry leading cloud-based transportation management platforms that often encompass passenger and driver applications as well as rules-based trip scheduling engines that optimize trip routing, reservations, dispatch, live trip tracking, and detailed reporting.

MV Vision

We will deliver the best customer experience with industry-leading safety, reliability, and innovation.

OPERATIONAL EXPERTISE

MV is a safe pair of hands for your transportation needs with a proven track record of more than 40 years and over 250 active engagements with expertise in scheduling, dispatch, maintenance, and driver operations.

SAFETY & TRAINING

We place the safety and security of our passengers, employees, and communities above all else, fortified by a rigorous, integrated cross-MV system of policies, procedures and programs, and regimented driver curricula.

MAINTENANCE

With a four decades-long commitment to providing safe, reliable, and clean transit fleets, MV maintains best practices in the industry related to data-driven preventative and predictive maintenance.

TECHNOLOGY & INTEGRATION

MV is a leader in leveraging advanced technology to optimize safety and efficiency. We maintain deep expertise with leading transit technologies, as well as MV’s proven, proprietary on-demand platform.
